How It Works

The art & science of reflexology

Reflexology is the holistic understanding, study and practice of treating points and areas in the feet and hands that relate to corresponding parts of the body.

Using precise hand and finger techniques, a reflexologist may improve circulation, induce deep relaxation and help the body return to its natural state of balance.

Treatment

What to expect in a session

A reflexology treatment lasts approximately one hour. As it is the feet which are generally treated, you simply remove your socks and shoes and settle into a comfortable treatment chair.

Your reflexologist then uses their hands to apply precise pressure techniques to the feet, working specific points that correspond to all the organs and systems of the body.

Afterwards, any significant findings are discussed with you. Because the effects of reflexology are cumulative, many people find it beneficial to continue with regular treatments.
